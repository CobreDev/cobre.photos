Hi, I'm Cooper - also known as Cobre! I'm a hobbyist photographer with experience in multiple genres of photography, ranging from airshows and aircraft to nature and wildlife.

I love capturing and sharing the world through my lens, whether it's the thrilling moments of an airshow or the simple beauty of daily life.

My photography journey began with just an iPhone and a fantastic app called Halide. As my skills and passion grew, I upgraded to a Nikon DSLR and a couple of versatile lenses that allow me to capture the wide range of scenes I love.

Feel free to explore my portfolio and see the world through my eyes. Follow me on social media or drop me a message - I'd love to hear from you!
